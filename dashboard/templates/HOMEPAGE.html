Climate Data Initiative (CDI)

The Climate Collection on Data.gov originated from the Climate Data Initiative (CDI), which was an effort to make federal climate datasets more accessible.

The CDI Analysis Platform (CAP) was developed to automate the analysis and maintenance of the data.gov Climate Collection through preserving the integrity of the originally curated CDI Masterlist.
